# Mission: Responsible AI

## Why
I want to understand responsible AI principles so I can later apply them to projects, tools, or decisions that use AI. The goal is not just to know the definitions, but to ask the right questions before a system causes harm.

## Success looks like
- Explain the six responsible AI principles in simple terms.
- Identify the main risks of an AI use case before deployment.
- Turn each principle into concrete controls: data, model, interface, human oversight, monitoring.
- Build a small audit sheet that can be used on a real project.

## Constraints
- Learn in English.
- Start with short, practical lessons.
- Rely on trusted sources rather than general opinions.

## Out of scope
- Produce a complete legal analysis of the AI Act.
- Jump straight into advanced model mathematics.
