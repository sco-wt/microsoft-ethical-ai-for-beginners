What You Will Practice

1. Name the use case Clarify which decision or recommendation AI influences.
2. Map affected people Identify who benefits, who loses, and who may be underrepresented.
3. Describe risks Make plausible harms explicit before deployment.
4. Choose safeguards Ask for evidence: tests, thresholds, human review, and appeal paths.
