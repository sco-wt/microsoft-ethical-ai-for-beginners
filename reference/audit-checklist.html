Responsible AI Audit Checklist

Course Home

Use this checklist when you want to move from responsible AI principles to concrete evidence.

Checklist

Area Ask Look for
Use case Which decision or recommendation does AI influence? Workflow description, human role, deployment context.
People Who is affected directly, indirectly, or invisibly? Stakeholder list, vulnerable groups, edge cases.
Fairness Do outcomes or errors differ across groups? Group-level metrics, failure examples, mitigation plan.
Reliability Where does the system fail, and how severe are those failures? Error analysis, stress tests, confidence thresholds.
Privacy Where can sensitive data enter, persist, or leak? Data map, minimization choices, retention policy.
Inclusion Who might be excluded from using or challenging the system? Accessibility review, language coverage, user research.
Transparency Do people know AI is involved and what its limits are? Disclosure, explanation, model limitations, user-facing copy.
Accountability Who can correct, pause, or take responsibility for the system? Named owner, appeal path, incident process, shutdown trigger.
Monitoring How will the team know whether the system remains acceptable? Metrics, alerts, review calendar, incident log.
